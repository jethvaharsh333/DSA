Number of subarrays whose sum <= K
(All numbers in the array>=0)

-> [2 1 1 5 8]
K = 4
There are 6 subarrays whose sum<=K :- [0…0] , [0….1] , [0….2] ,[1…1],[2..2],[1….2]
